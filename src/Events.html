Events

I attend several local events on a monthly basis. Below you will find my current events.

ArtLimerick Pop up

Apr 21st - 23rd

ArtLimerick Pop up shop, at 19 Nicholas St, Limerick city.

Friday 11 to 4.30 and Saturday and Sunday from 11 to 5.30pm. If you are out and about in the Medieval Quarter of Limerick come in and say hi! We are opposite Katie Daly's, just down from the castle and Treaty City Brewery.

5 different artists and crafters every weekend showcasing their work. All work is for sale.

Limerick Potato Market

Apr 30th

Limerick Potato Market. We are part of Riverfest 2023, we will be beside the Panoramic Wheel. Artists and makers from ArtLimerick will be displaying work.

Sunday 11am to 4pm.

Come into town, soak up some art and have a nice cuppa too. No better way to spend a Sunday.
