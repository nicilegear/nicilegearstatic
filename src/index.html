About me

Beautiful things painted with acrylic on canvas.

Story

I have always had an interest in art. From a very young age I would spend my days drawing. I am a self taught artist, after working many years in Digital as a User Experience Designer. I love painting, and feel that it allows me to completely relax and get in the zone. I am exploring styles and types of painting.
I have original paintings, as well as archival quality prints for sale. If any painting takes your fancy please do get in touch.
