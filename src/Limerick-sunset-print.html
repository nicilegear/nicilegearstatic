Limerick Sunset Print

Limited Edition print.
Size is 10 x 11 inches.

Mounted archival print, to conservation quality.

A gorgeous sunset spotted on a late June evening this summer on the river Shannon. The aspect is looking towards Thomond Bridge with King John's Castle on the right.

All prints are on Epson Somerset Velvet archival fine art paper with 13 chromatic pigment inks. The paper is completely archival and 100% acid free which means it will not discolour or tarnish with ageing. The prints are also light resistant for up to 150 years.
